# ListenAR Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

ListenAR Translate does **not** run servers that store your conversations. We do not sell your data.

## The short version

- **Translation history and favorites** stay **on your device only** (iPhone, Apple Watch, or Android).
- Your **OpenRouter API key** (if you add one) is stored in your device **Keychain** (iOS) or encrypted app storage (Android)—not on ListenAR servers.
- **Speech and photos** are processed **on your device** using Apple or Google on-device technologies unless you turn on optional AI (see below).
- **No transcripts** are sent to ListenAR—we have no backend that receives your translations.
- **Feedback** is sent **only when you choose** to send it from Settings → Feedback (e.g. to help fix bugs). You can send without attaching logs.

## Optional features (your choice)

| Feature | What happens |
|--------|----------------|
| **AI translation** | If you enable it and add your own OpenRouter API key, **text** (not raw audio) may be sent to **OpenRouter** under their privacy policy. ListenAR does not host that service. |
| **Diagnostic logs** | Optional on-device logs (errors, timings)—**not** your translation text. You can turn saving off, export, or clear in Settings. |
| **Feedback message** | May include your name, message, device model, app version, and optional log attachment—only when **you** tap Send. |

## Third-party services

Apple (speech, translation, camera on iOS), Google (ML Kit, speech on Android), and OpenRouter (optional AI) process data according to **their** policies when you use those features. ListenAR does not control those services.

## Children

ListenAR is not directed at children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child submitted personal information through Feedback.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app grows (e.g. when we add more languages). The “Last updated” date above will change when we do.
